The origin of Title

The letter H had been adopted by Greek from the Phoenician letter Heth (inline) originally with this consonantal sound value, and Hēta was its original name. The Italic alphabets, and ultimately Latin, adopted the letter H from this Greek usage.

However, Greek dialects progressively lost the sound /h/ from their phonological systems. In the Ionic dialects, where this loss of /h/ happened early, the name of the letter naturally changed to Ēta.

As Hēta was its original name, and hour section is 'H' which came from HETA, that's why I enttitled the name of the project as "The HETA Project"
